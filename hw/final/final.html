Our Story

In name at least, there had always been a Juventus Club at the Football Fcatory at Legends in New York City. But the club was not organized well, and turnouts for games were low. Six people were inspired to change the culture, knowing that a prestigious team such as Juventus could produce a much larger and more engaging community in New York City. Working together, those six fans spent months laying the groundwork for a new and improved fan club. In 2015, we hosted our first watch party under the new leadership.Two years later after facing trials and tribulations, we were able to to succesfully become Juventus Official Fan Club Empire State. Uniting with smaller fan clubs across the city to create one.

WHO WE ARE

At Juventus Official Fan Club Empire State, we have a bevy of ways to get invovlevd and feel like you are a part of a special tightknit community. Along with hosting watch parties every Sunday during the Serie A season, we also host special match day experiences at Football Factory in Legends during the Champions League. By joining the club you also gain priority access to Special Juventus Events, whether thats early access to match day tickets, meeting players, attaining new merch, and much more.
